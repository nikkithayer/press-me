Okay so these are the currently written up mission ideas.

Phase 0:
Introduce yourself to at least three people and enter their real names and get them to sign off using their passphrase.

Phase I
1. Operation Live to Serve- Dress up in the waiter uniform and serve another guest food and/or drink then get them to confirm the service. Bounty: 200
2. Operation House Always Wins- Put on a disguise and run the gambling table until you get a tip from another guest. Bounty: 100
3. Operation Thin Blue Liar- Get the police badge and interogate your hosts for information on the secret discovery. Bounty: 300
4. Operation MLM- Disguise yourself as a wealthy patron and convince a guest to give at least 500 to our hosts to invest in the toy company. Bounty: 300
5. Operation New Hire- Create a new cover identity with an identifiable profession and get another guest to sign off that you seem like an upstanding member of whatever profession you've chosen. Bounty: 250

Act II

6. Operation Lost Shirt- Go play at the gambling table and lose at least 500. Bounty: 200
7. Operation False Fracas- Start a fight with [other assigned agent] until one of the hosts comes to check on you. Bounty: 250
8. Operation Don't I know you?- Get another guest to give you their autograph and then show it to your hosts. Bounty: 100
9. Operation But Is It Art?- Wear the beret and convince another guest to buy your "art" for at least 300 then get them to fill out a form that they've done so. Bounty: 100
10. Operation Prodigal Son- Wear a new disguise and convince the same mark you found in Operation New Hire that you're a long lost relative. Bounty: 400

Act III

11. Operation Foreign Investment- Don a new disguise and pose as a foreign investor with an outrageous accent to convince the hosts that they need to act normal for the "foreign investors" Bounty: 500
12. Operation Stop Where You Are- Disguise yourself as the police again and deescelate the situation with the hosts. Bounty: 500
13. Operation Over There- Create a distraction so the current holder of The Technology can hide it or pass it to another guest. Bounty: 500
